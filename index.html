Most Popular Cuisenes of Bangladesh

Biriyani

Biryani in Bangladesh is a beloved dish, often enjoyed during special occasions, celebrations, and festivals. The Bangladeshi version is known for its rich flavors and aromatic spices. One of the most famous types is Kacchi Biryani, which features marinated raw meat layered with partially cooked rice, then sealed and cooked together, allowing the flavors to blend.

Panta Illish

Panta Ilish is a traditional Bengali dish that is especially popular in Bangladesh and the Indian state of West Bengal. It consists of panta bhat (fermented rice) served with ilish, a highly prized river fish known for its rich flavor and delicate texture.
